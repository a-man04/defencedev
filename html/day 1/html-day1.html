How to Become a Frontend Developer

Who is a Frontend Developer?

A Frontend Developer is a professional who specialises in creating the user-facing part of a website-everything a person sees and intracts with in their browese. They are the architects of the digital experrience, responsible for turning a static design into a living, breathing, and interactive webpage.
